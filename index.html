Frequently Asked Questions

What brands do you specialize in?

We specialize in servicing both foreign and domestic luxury and premium vehicles. Our technicians receive ongoing, brand-specific training to ensure the highest quality service.

Do you use genuine OEM parts?

Absolutely. We exclusively use genuine OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts or manufacturer-approved equivalents. We never use aftermarket parts unless specifically requested by the customer, and even then we advise against it for luxury vehicles.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a comprehensive 24-month/24,000-mile warranty on all repairs and services. This is one of the most generous warranties in the industry and reflects our confidence in the quality of our workmanship.

Do you offer loaner vehicles?

Yes, we provide complimentary loaner vehicles for services that require more than 2 hours. Our loaner fleet includes late-model luxury vehicles to ensure your experience remains premium from start to finish.

Can I wait at your facility?

Certainly! Our customer lounge features premium leather seating, complimentary gourmet coffee and refreshments, high-speed WiFi, and a viewing area where you can watch our technicians work on your vehicle through large glass windows.

How do I know what service my car needs?

Our digital service advisors will perform a complimentary multi-point inspection and provide you with a detailed report, including photos and videos of any areas of concern. We'll explain all recommended services and let you decide what work to authorize.
